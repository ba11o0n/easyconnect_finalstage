About EasyConnect

EasyConnect was founded with a vision to make networking at events intuitive, instant, and impactful. We believe that meaningful professional connections should happen seamlessly—without apps, QR codes, or awkward moments.

With our smart wearable tech, professionals, students, and businesses can interact through a natural gesture: the handshake. We strive to bridge the gap between the physical and digital world through thoughtful design and secure technology.

Our team is composed of passionate developers, engineers, and event specialists who are committed to redefining how people connect.

Core Features

  • 🤝 Smart wristband with NFC & BLE support
  • 📇 Instantly shares professional profiles
  • 📱 No app installation required
  • 📊 Dashboard for vendors, organizers, and attendees

How It Works
